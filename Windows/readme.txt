How to use Car Permission Windows.

1. Download the folder as Zip file into your computer and extract it
2. Edit CarPermissionMaster.xlsx to  your requirements
3. In config.txt, change "To_mail = example@bajajauto.co.in" to set email to your email address
4. Edit signature.txt to put your signature at the end of the email
5. Double click on CarPermissionBot.exe to send mail to your address
6. Open Outlook,select the recieved email and set Rules to auto forward this message to recipients in Transport department
7. All done, enjoy


Optional: in config.txt edit days_offset parameter to change the date of permission relative to current date
